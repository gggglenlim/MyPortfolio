Reading is one of my passions. It helps me learn, imagine, and escape into different worlds. Whether it's a novel, manga, or inspirational book, I always find joy in flipping through the pages. I love reading comics, manga, and webtoons because they bring stories to life in a unique way—combining art and words to create worlds I can dive into. Each panel feels like stepping into a different universe, letting me experience emotions, adventures, and lessons through vibrant characters and beautiful storytelling. They're not just entertainment for me, but a way to escape, imagine, and connect with creativity on a deeper level.

Taekwondo keeps me active, disciplined, and focused. It is more than just a sport—it's a journey of growth. I started my training in Good Tree Church, where I met new friends and built bonds that made the experience even more meaningful. At first, I didn’t really like it, but as time went on, I slowly fell in love with the sport. It helps me stay physically fit and active, while also allowing me to connect and share experiences with others. Beyond the physical benefits, Taekwondo has helped me grow mentally and emotionally, teaching me discipline, patience, and resilience. It fuels my competitive spirit, pushing me to challenge myself and aim higher every time I step on the mat.
